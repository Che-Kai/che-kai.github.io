About Me

Hello, I am a 2nd year Ph.D. student advised by Professor Arijit Raychowdhury at the Integrated Circuits and Systems Research Lab, School of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Georgia Institute of Technology. My current research focus at GaTech and TSMC is on mixed-signal physical chip and architecture design for next-generation AI workloads.
